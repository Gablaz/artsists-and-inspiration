Michelangelo

Michelangelo (March 6,1475-February 18, 1564) was an italian sculptor, painter, architect madn poet. He was considered one of the greatest artists in his lifetime due to the amount of his works ranking among the most famous in existence. Michelangelo saw himself primarily as a sculptor and hardly any of his other traits even though the art that he painted on the ceiling of the sistine chapel was one of his best works. He primarily worked with marble in his sculptures, some of his famous works include Angel, Tomb of Pope Julius II, and The Pieta.
