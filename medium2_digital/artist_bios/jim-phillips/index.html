Jim Phillips

Jim Phillips is a graphic artist that is best known for his skateboard, rock, and surf art. Phillips was born in San Jose in 1944 but was living the majority of his time in Santa Cruz. His early jobs were in various surfboard manufacturing shops and applying art and designs to the surfboards. Phillips created 100+ rock posters since 1967 for the likes of the Doors, Jimi Hendrix, and the Surfaris.
