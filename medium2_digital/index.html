This form of art can be traced back to 15,000 B.C. Graphic design is the art of arranging visual elements in order to convey a message. Jim Phillips is a graphic artist that is best known for his skateboard, rock, and surf art. Wolfgang Weingart is an internationally known graphic designer and typographer credited as "the father" of New Wave or Swiss Punk typography. Rino Russo is a web artist who started out as a graphic designer. David Belliveau is a Canadian artist known for his expressive digital illustrations. Lester Beall was an American graphic designer best known for his work on the Rural Electrification Administration posters which was honored by MoMA.
