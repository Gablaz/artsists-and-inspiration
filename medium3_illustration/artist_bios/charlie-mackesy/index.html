Charlie Mackesy

Charlie Mackesy is a British artist and author born in Northumberland, United Kingdom in 1962. He never attended art school and instead spent three months learning from an American portrait painter. Mackesy started his career off by working as a cartoonist for The Spectator and then moved on to become a book illustrator for Oxford University Press. His work was eventually taken on by galleries and had his first exhibition for his illustrations in London at The Park Walk Gallery and has since had numerous other exhibitions in London, Edinburgh and New York.
