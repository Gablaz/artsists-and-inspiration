Hayao Miyazaki

Hayao Miyazaki was born on January 5, 1941 in Tokyo, Japan. Miyazaki is a japanese illustrator and director and has created multiple japanese films that are very well known to this day. In 1963, Miyazaki obtained a position as an entry-level animator in Toei Animation. In Toei, he met fellow animators by the name of Takahata Isao and Ota Akemi where they became very good friends and later on became business partners. Takahata and Miyazaki undertook a more permanent partnership and launched Studio Ghibli together in 1985. They started out with Castle in the Sky and later on released their other known films such as "My neighbor Totoro" and "Princess Mononoke".
