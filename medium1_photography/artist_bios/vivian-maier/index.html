Vivian Maier

Vivian Maier was born February 1, 1926. During her forties, she worked as a nanny in 1950’s Chicago. In her free time, she roamed the street photographing the architecture, people, and various events of the time period. Unfortunately, thousands of Maier’s prints and negatives were only discovered in her re-acclaimed storage locker in 2007. Shortly after her death in 2009, her work became a worldwide phenomenon.
